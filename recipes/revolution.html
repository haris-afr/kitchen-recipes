Revolution

The overthrow of the oppressing class by the oppresed classes, with the use of extreme force

Ingredients

  • Contradictions
  • Ruling class
  • Lower class(es)

Recipe

  1. Look for contradictions within the dominant system
  2. If contradictions exist, wait for those contradictions to lead to a breaking point
  3. In the meanwhile, organise a vanguard party to overthrow the dominant system
  4. Connect with the lower classes, educate, agitate, organise
  5. When the time comes, Seize the means
  6. Win
